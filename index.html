For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to desolve. Halve all the figs and toss in trhe saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you have a chunky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one or twi stirs, set asside and let cool completely

Ice Cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granulateds sugar until sugaer is completely desolved and the milk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. Set mixture in the fridge and wait until the fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to manufacturer instrucxtions, i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding marscapone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Contenue to process for +/- 10 minues. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.

  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup mascarpone
  • 1/3 granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp pink salt
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1lb Black Mission Figs
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2tbsp butter
  • 2-4 tbsp water
  • 1 cup honey roasted pecans,roughly chopped
